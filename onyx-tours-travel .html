Gorilla Trekking Experience

Trek Into the Misty Mountains — Meet the Giants

A gorilla trek is a highlight of all visits done in Uganda and Rwanda. Trekking can be conducted year-round in Uganda, Rwanda and Congo. The best months are June, July, August, September and December.

Our expert guides have over a decade of trekking experience, ensuring your safety, comfort and an unforgettable encounter with these gentle giants at close range.

Year-Round Treks

Available every month in Rwanda, Uganda and DRC with expert naturalist guides.

Permits Arranged

We secure your gorilla permits in advance — they sell out fast. Leave it to us.

Small Groups

Maximum 8 visitors per gorilla family for a truly intimate and responsible encounter.

Conservation First

A portion of every trek fee goes directly to mountain gorilla conservation.

The Great Migration

The World's Greatest Wildlife Show — On Your Doorstep

Every year, over 1.5 million wildebeest and 500,000 zebra and gazelle undertake a 1,800-mile circular journey through Tanzania's Serengeti and Kenya's Maasai Mara — crossing crocodile-filled rivers in terrifying, breath-taking river crossings.

Onyx positions you in the heart of the action, with expert guides who know exactly where the herds will be on any given day.

River Crossings

The dramatic Mara River crossings are the iconic highlight — we know where and when.

Year-Round Action

Calving season in January, river crossings in August — there's always something spectacular.

Private Vehicles

No shared jeeps — your own 4WD and guide, positioned exactly where you want to be.

Luxury Camps

Wake up with the sounds of the herd from your private tented suite on the Mara.
